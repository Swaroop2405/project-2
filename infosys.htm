    Infosys

    Glassdoor – 3.7

    The big daddy of all IT companies, Infosys leads the list of the top IT companies in Bangalore. Infosys has around 10 offices in the city in areas such as Electronic City and Hosur Road.

    Top rated employee perks are

    • Paternity and Maternity Leave
    • Health Care and Insurance
    • Job Training and Tuition

     

    Accenture

    Glassdoor – 3.8

    Accenture is one of the largest management consulting companies in the world that is headquartered in Ireland. They are one of the first MNCs to step foot in India and have 20 offices in Bangalore.

    Top rated employee perks are

    • Vacation and Paid Time Off
    • Maternity and Paternity Leave
    • Sick Leave

     

    Wipro

    Glassdoor – 3.5

    Wipro was initially a cooking oil manufacturing company and under the leadership of Azim Premji, they have become a technological giant. Their corporate office is in Sarjapur

    Top rated employee perks are

    • Maternity and Paternity Leave
    • Sick Leave
    • Vacation and Paid Time Off

     

    TCS

    Glassdoor – 3.7

    Tata Consultancy Services is one of Indias oldest IT companies and is also one of Indias largest companies in terms of capitalization.

    Top rated employee perks are

    • Paid Sick Leave
    • Education/Training/Tuition/Certification Reimbursement
    • Life Insurance/Disability
    • Paid Holidays / Vacation
    • Gym / Health Club / Fitness Membership
    • Flex-Time / Flexible Schedule
    • Casual Dress/Atmosphere

     

    IBM

    Glassdoor – 3.6


    IBMs history spans over 100 years and they have an outsourcing team in Bangalore that works on R&D projects as well

    Top rated employee perks are

    • Paid Sick Leave
    • Paid Holidays
    • Work from Home

     

    Oracle

    Glassdoor – 3.6

    Oracle is the biggest database software and technology company in the world and they have a huge presence in India and has over 10 offices in Bangalore in areas such as Marathahalli and C.V. Raman Nagar among others.

    Top rated employee perks are

    • Work from Home
    • Casual Dress/Atmosphere
    • Free juice and drinks
    • Paid Sick Leave

     

    Cognizant

    Glassdoor – 3.6

    Contrary to popular belief, Cognizant Technology Solutions is an American company registered in New Jersey. Its ranked 198 in the Fortune 500 list and has 4 offices in Bangalore.

    Top rated employee perks are

    • Paid sick leave
    • Paid holidays
    • Life Insurance

     

    Capgemini

    Glassdoor – 3.3

    Capgemini is big on consulting and technology services and they also have an outsourcing arm. Capgemini has over 10 offices in India.

    Top rated employee perks are

    • Paid Vacation
    • Paid Sick Leave
    • Life Insurance
    • Flexible Schedules

     

    Cisco

    Glassdoor – 4.0

    Ciscos journey began in 1984 and since then it has gone on to become a networking hardware and a telecommunications giant. Cisco has 7 offices in Bangalore.

    Top rated employee perks are

    • Paid Vacation and Time off
    • Work from Home
    • Paternity and Maternity Leave

     

    Mindtree

    Glassdoor – 3.6

    Mindtree was founded by the iconic Subroto Bagchi and a few others in 1999. It employes 19,000 employees all over the world and has a massive presence in Bangalore where it has 5 offices.

    Top rated employee perks are

    • Health Insurance
    • Maternity and Paternity Leave
    • Vacation and Paid Time off

     

    HCL

    Glassdoor – 3.3

    HCL was founded by Shiv Nadar and is one of Indias top 5 IT firms. It has 5 offices in Bangalore and the biggest one is located in Electronic City.

    Top rated employee perks are

    • Life Insurance
    • Paid Sick Leave
    • Cell Phone

     

    Mu Sigma

    Glassdoor – 3.4

    Mu Sigma is not your typical IT company. They work on Decision Sciences and Big Data Analytics. They help customers make sense of their data that can improve performance metrics. Mu Sigma has a large office in Ascendas ITPL.

    Top rated employee perks are

    • Free beverages
    • Paid Sick Leave
    • Health Care and Insurance

     

    Amazon

    Glassdoor – 4.1

    Amazon, the e-commerce giant started their operations in India in 2014 and have gone on to capture a big market share in India. Amazon has development centres in Hyderabad also apart from Bangalore

    Top rated employee perks are

    • Health Insurance
    • Vacation and Paid Time Off
    • Work from Home
    • Maternity and Paternity Leave

     

    Dell

    Glassdoor – 3.9

    Dell, the personal computer giant recently went private and are one of the most popular laptop companies in India. Dell has an office in EPIP Zone in Whitefield, Bangalore

    Top rated employee perks are

    • Referral Bonus Program
    • On-site gym
    • On-site cafe
    • Mother’s room
    • Sick Leave
    • Work from Home

     

    HP

    Glassdoor – 3.9

    Hewlett Packard, the computer giant has retail outlets in India where they sell personal computers and also have offices in Bagmane Tech Park and RMZ Ecospace

    Top rated employee perks are

    • Vacation and Paid Time Off
    • Employee Discount
    • Maternity and Paternity Leave

     

    Tech Mahindra

    Glassdoor – 3.2

    Tech Mahindra, which was formed after the merger of Satyam Computers and Mahindra make the top ten list of one of Indias top homegrown IT companies

    Top rated employee perks are

    • Health Insurance
    • Vacation and Paid Time Off
    • Work from Home

     

    SAP

    Glassdoor – 4.5

    SAP is the global leader in Enterprise Resource Planning and have a suite of applications and tools that help businesses scale. SAP has a large and sprawling office in Whitefield, that is one of many offices in Bangalore.

    Top rated employee perks are

    • Free Beverages
    • Paid sick leave
    • Work from home
    • Life insurance

     

    Samsung R&D

    Glassdoor – 4.5

    Samsung, the Korean giant has a R&D centre in Bangalore that hire developers in Bangalore for building the technology for new products

    Top rated employee perks are

    • Maternity and Paternity Leave
    • Free Lunch / Snacks
    • Health Care and Insurance

     

    Robert Bosch

    Glassdoor – 3.6

    Bosch is an engineering conglomerate that has a presence in various industries such as Mobility Solutions, Industrial Technology among others. They have 5 offices in Bangalore.

    Top rated employee perks are

    • Maternity and Paternity Leave
    • Free Lunch or Snacks
    • Vacation and Paid Time Off

     

    Thomson Reuters

    Glassdoor – 3.8

    Thomson Reuters is the holding company that has various businesses. From Media to Tax Compliance software, they have a wide range of products. Thomson Reuters has 3 offices in India.

    Top rated employee perks are

    • Health Insurance
    • Vacation and Paid Time Off

     

    Honeywell

    Glassdoor – 3.4

    Honeywell is a 112 year old company that manufacture various consumer facing products as well as equipments for the aerospace industry. Honeywell has 4 main offices in Bangalore.

    Top rated employee perks are

    • Paid Sick Leave
    • Supplemental Maternity Leave
    • Health Insurance
    • Paid Vacation

     

    CGI

    Glassdoor – 3.4

    CGI is a Canadian IT consulting firm that has a presence in a wide range of industries such as manufacturing, logistics and retail. They have 3 main offices in Bangalore.

    Top rated employee perks are

    • Maternity and Paternity Leave
    • Vacation and Paid Time Off
    • Work from Home

     

    Mphasis

    Glassdoor – 3.4

    Mphasis is an IT services company with its headquarters in Bangalore. They do a variety of services such as architecture guidance and application development. They have 9 offices in Bangalore.

    Top rated employee perks are

    • Sick Leave
    • Paid Time Off
    • Maternity and Paternity Leave

     

    EY

    Glassdoor – 3.7

    Ernst and Young although is an accounting firm, has a technology arm that build products for internal and external use

    Top rated employee perks are

    • Health Insurance
    • Maternity Leave

     

    Deloitte

    Glassdoor – 3.8

    Although Deloitte is a management consulting and accounting firm, it hires a lot of developers for its various services and products. Deloitte has 4 main offcies in Bangalore.

    Top rated employee perks are

    • Work From Home
    • Paid Vacation
    • Maternity and Paternity leave

     

    Nokia

    Glassdoor – 4.2

     


    Nokia, the Finnish mobile giant has made inroads in the smartphone market with their Android smartphones. They hire developers for their Bangalore office to build products for the world.

    Top rated employee perks are

    • Cell Phone
    • Life Insurance
    • Paid SIck Leave

     

    Intel

    Glassdoor – 3.8

    The largest computer chip maker in the world, Intel has had a presence in India for well over a decade. Intel has 5 offices in Bangalore in areas such as Brookfield and Indiranagar.

    Top rated employee perks are

    • Maternity Leave (2 Weeks)
    • Paternity Leave (6 Weeks)
    • Work from Home
    • Vacation and Paid Time Off

     

    Huawei

    Glassdoor – 3.4

    Huawei is the Chinese Mobile Phone manufacturer that sell their Honor and Huawei brands of phones in India. They also have offices in India, with 3 in Bangalore. There is a small Huawei R&D unit in Whitefield.

    Top rated employee perks are

    • Performance Bonus
    • Health Care and Insurance
    • Sick Leave

     

    Goldman Sachs

    Glassdoor – 3.9

    Goldman Sachs is an investment banking firm that has a huge presence in India with them being one of the biggest MNC hirers. They have 5 offices in Bangalore.

    Top rated employee perks are

    • Vacation and Paid Time Off
    • Maternity Leave (2 Months)
    • Paternity Leave (6 Months)
    • Sick Leave

     

    Flipkart

    Glassdoor – 4.2

    Flipkart which is Indias first recognized startup is one of the largest e-commerce companies in India have multiple offices in Bangalore and are headquartered in Koramangala.

    Top rated employee perks are

    • Maternity and Paternity Leave
    • Vacation and Paid Time Off
    • Work from Home

    Best IT Companies to work for in Bangalore

    All these companies always are part of the Top 50 IT companies in Bangalore list.

    Bangalores IT juggernaut doesnt look like its going to stop with many more new age companies making its foray into the garden city. Retail giants like Ikea and Target have outsourced their operations to Bangalore and operate large teams.

    If you are looking for work opportunities in the IT field, there is not a bigger work field in India apart from Bangalore.
